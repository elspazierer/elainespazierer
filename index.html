Summary

Gigi Hadid, born Jelena Noura Hadid on April 23, 1995, is an American supermodel and fashion icon who has left an indelible mark on the global fashion industry.

Raised in Los Angeles, California, Gigi hails from a multicultural background, with a Dutch mother and Palestinian father, giving her a unique and captivating look.
